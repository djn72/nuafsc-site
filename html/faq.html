The Newcastle United Armed Forces Supporters Club

The Suppporters Club has was founded for the purpose of supporting its members of the Armed Forces and Veterans, with the one common love of our club, Newcastle United.

We are hoping to forge a connection with Newcastle United in the future and are already looking to assist with the Remembrance Service at St James Park next year in 2024.
